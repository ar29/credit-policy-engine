Policy Document: MSME Express Loan Program (v2.1)
Confidential - For Internal Use Only

1. Applicant Eligibility
--------------------------
• Business Vintage: To ensure stability, the borrowing entity must have been 
  operational for a minimum of 24 months from the official date of registration.
• Age Criteria: The primary applicant or promoter must be at least 21 years 
  old at the time of application. Furthermore, the applicant's age must not 
  exceed 65 years at the point of loan maturity (end of the loan tenure).
• Nationality: Eligibility is strictly limited to Indian Citizens residing 
  within the domestic borders of India.

2. Financial Requirements
--------------------------
• Minimum Annual Turnover: The business must demonstrate scale by showing a 
  minimum audited or GST-certified annual turnover of ₹15,00,000.
• Banking Stability: We require bank statements for the previous 6 months. 
  The application will be flagged for review if there are more than 2 cheque 
  bounces due to "insufficient funds" within the last 90 days.
• Debt-to-Income (DTI): To prevent over-leveraging, the Total Monthly 
  Obligations (including the proposed loan EMI) must not exceed a 50% ratio 
  of the Net Monthly Income (FOIR).

3. Credit Scoring (CIBIL)
--------------------------
• Standard Approval: A minimum CIBIL Bureau score of 700 is mandatory for 
  unsecured loans up to ₹10,00,000.
• High-Value Tier: For loan requests exceeding ₹10,00,000, the risk appetite 
  tightens to a minimum CIBIL requirement of 750.
• New-to-Credit: Applicants with a score of "-1" or "0" (No History) may 
  be considered for approval only if they provide a co-applicant with a 
  demonstrated CIBIL score exceeding 720.

4. Negative Industry List
--------------------------
The following sectors are designated as "High Risk" and applications must 
be Rejected immediately:
• Real Estate developers and Brokers.
• Gem and Jewellery wholesalers.
• Perishable agricultural goods trading (unless Cold-Storage proof is provided).

5. Loan Purpose & Tenure
--------------------------
• Maximum Tenure: 36 months for working capital requirements; 60 months 
  for specific machinery or asset purchases.
• Restricted Use: Loan proceeds are strictly for business growth. Use for 
  speculative investments, stock market trading, or repayment of personal 
  credit card debt is prohibited.
